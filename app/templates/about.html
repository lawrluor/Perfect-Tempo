What is "Perfect Tempo?"

"Perfect Tempo" is the theorized ability of humans to assign specific BPMs to rhythmic patterns, much like absolute pitch ("perfect pitch").

What is "Perfect Tempo?" (The Game)

Perfect Tempo is a game that is designed to help you practice your tempo. It is not prescriptive.

What sound samples are you using?

A midi drumset patch.

Can I change the instrument type?

At the moment, no.
